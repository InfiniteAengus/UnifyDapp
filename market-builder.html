Market Builder

The Unifty Market Builder is an easy interface that creates dedicated marketplaces which are decentralized and creator owned and controlled. Rather than relying on centralized services, or predefined rule sets, creators can build their own custom markets with their own fee rules, and rules on which NFTs can be bought and sold on their marketplace. Market Builder integrates NIF based staking to ensure that marketplaces fully align with the goals of the wider Unifty ecosystem, and to keep creators incentivised to maintain their community.
